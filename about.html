About Union SCM

Union SCM Co., Ltd. was established in December 2015 with the headquarters in Taipei, Taiwan and branch in Shanghai, China.

Repair product lines are located at the Free Trade Zone, Taoyuan, Taiwan and Kun Shan City, Jiang Su Province , China. We perform repair service for both bonded and non-bond products.

We focus on rework, refurbish and repair for DT & SVR & NB PCBA / GPS PND / Smart Phone / Pad /LCD Panel associated with electronic and consumer products.

We also can do Spare Parts trading and worldwide on-side Technical Support and we have strategic repair partners in India, Europe, N. America and S. America to serve customers’ needs.

We have the most advanced IT system to manage working processes related to Warehouse Management, RMA, Rework and Repair etc.
